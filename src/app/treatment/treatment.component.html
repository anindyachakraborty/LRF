Our Treatment Process

BEST DE- ADDICTION CUM REHABILITATION CENTER

Accomodation

Life Revision Foundation Provide Accommodation to all of the addicted patients. Accommodation type will be Lobby, Dormitory, AC and Non AC.

Yoga and Meditation

The calm breathing exercises, gentle motions, and meditative benefits of yoga can help calm your nerves, reduce cravings, and help treat any psychological distress.

Consciousness Classes

Consciousness classes help you learn more about you and your life and provides you the knowledge to help you stay clean and make you aware of addiction.

Physical Check Up

We all know addiction affects our body in a negative way. So in rehabilitation process, physical check up becomes a necessary part when dealing with recovery to understand your physical problems and take steps to cure those.

Indoor Games and Hobbies

This part is completely for your recreation purpose. We want you to be in a place where you feel comfortable and happy and that’s why our rehab center is located near Garia in a open environment in Chiramore, Kaikhali.

Counselling

Counselling in rehabilitation program provides you support, education to improve your motivation for recovery, to make you Learn the skills for stopping alcohol abuse and avoiding relapse and many more.

Psychiatric Treatment

Addiction doesn’t only harms our body. It also harms our brain. To make you mentally healthy we take you through this treatment process as a part of our addiction treatment program.

Recreation and Celebrations

Recovery and Stay in Abstinance from any mood altering substance is a journey not a destination. And we know any journey without any sort of enjoyment is very painfull.

Community Living

We know the man is by nature a social animal. We can not live alone by ourselves only. But in our active using period we actually tend to practice that.
